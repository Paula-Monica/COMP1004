Information:

This website has been created to support students with their time managment skills. You are able to select the times at which you're available to work and input any tasks you must complete. Input the time you wish to spend on each task per week and move it into your timetable. Alongside this there are features to support you with productive work such as a built in pomodoro timer.

Make a difference to your studies with the use of time scheduling technologies.
